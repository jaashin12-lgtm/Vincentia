Thai Massage

“Be Relax” is located in Burton Street shopping village. Most popular treatments: traditional Thai massage, organic coconut oil massage, and body scrub with hot towels.

Offers a range of treatments alongside Jasmine tea, prices ranging from $69-$190.

Creative Workshops

Embark on a creative journey through events, such as Watercolor painting, Stencil, Sketching, Spray painting, Oil painting, and Canvas painting workshop in Vincentia.

Bring your family and friends for a fun art experience many of which are free of charge

Bike Hire

At “Jervis Bay Bike Hire” they offer, bicycles, including beach cruisers, hybrids, tandems, and trikes, and provide delivery and pickup services to your location.

Enjoy a family or group bike ride, in costumes around Vincentia’s Parks

Jervis Bay Marine Park

Its natural beauty includes a beautiful underwater world in Jervis Bay Marine Park including: spot playful dolphins and migrating whales or just relax on exquisite beaches. 

Enjoy snorkeling looking out for turtles, seahorses, and stingrays in a whole group.

The Golfie

I renowned restaurant to be enjoyed in the nightlife, a hidden gem serving Thai, Chinese, Malaysian & daily specials, and some western dishes.

Enjoy lovely views over golf course and kangaroos roaming freely.

Kayaking

At Huskisson, enjoy guided trips, kayak hire and kayaking tutition, a whole kayaking trip!

Have fun at daily kayaking trips from $70 to $160 for tuition.

B/B Leisure Center

Have an active lifestyle? Enjoy a fully-equipped gym, 25m lap pool, spa, waterside and even personal training.

To swim as an adult only $7.80 and for a family, $27.30

Super Bargain

A variety discount store, for products such as home ware, kitchenware, party supplies (helium balloons) and seasonal products.

Plan a fun party event for any holiday, Christmas, Halloween, Weddings!

The Hippie shop

Purchase items such as pouches, Hippie fashion, incense, jewelry, gemstones and more. They have interesting items related to health and gift packaging as well.

Buy three pouches for $10 dollars, and wear a your new weekend day fit.

Burton St Eateries

Includes breakfast, coffee and brunch, Italian, Thai Chinese restaurants, Pizza’s and Kebabs and organic food store.

Explore the entire street morning to night, taking Instagram worthy pictures!

Shopping Village

Need to buy gift, groceries, toys, rather go to somewhere affordable to dine or discover a huge selection on health, beauty and more?

Try Coles click & collect system to make homemade dinners easy

Moona Creek Eateries

Try a new street, Authentic pad Thai and green curries, budget friendly noodles, artisan coffee, fish and chips.

Even options for vegetarians and gluten-free, and picnic by the sunset
